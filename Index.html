The Research Mandate

The study of violence has traditionally been bifurcated: micro-sociologists (like Randall Collins) examine the immediate situational dynamics of confrontation, while macro-analysts study structural forces using large-scale datasets.

Our objective is to fuse these perspectives. By leveraging real-time online data streams (ACLED, GDELT) and rigorous experimental designs (Survey Experiments, Quasi-Experimental Methods), we can model how macro-level shocks translate into micro-level aggressive behaviors.
